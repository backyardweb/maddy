About DDK

DDK was formed in 1973 to distribute heating and ventilation equipment to the construction industry in British Columbia. In May 2001, Gerry Sjodin retired and the next generation of Sjodin's took over: Roger Sjodin, with over 15 years in the ventilation industry, is President and manages an experienced team that includes sales, technical and operations specialists. Carmen Sjodin joined the company five years ago and handles the financial and administrative matters.
