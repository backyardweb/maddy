september 3rd-4th hike


Anyone is welcome to come, invite your friends. Get someone to add you, or message me and I will add you to a group chat.

what

A 29km two day hike and camping trip.

where

The Black Tusk (by Whistler)

Begin hike at Rubble Creek Trailhead

when

Sunday, September 3rd - Monday, September 4th (Labour Day long weekend)

Leave Sunday morning most likely around 9am (TBD), hike for roughly 7 hours, stay the night near the summit, return Monday.

bring
  • Enough water & food for yourself for 2 days of exercise.
  • A tent, or find someone who is bringing a tent that you can sleep in.
  • A sleeping bag and warm night time clothes (it is cold).
  • Good boots/shoes.
  • Clothing (layers).
  • Other hiking stuff.
